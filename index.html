Methodology

The five stages of GTD, built in.

Every screen, workflow, and shortcut is designed around David Allen's trusted system. Not a generic to-do app with GTD bolted on as an afterthought.

1

Capture

Brain Dump anything on your mind into the Inbox. AI coaches you through it, surfacing forgotten commitments and open loops you'd miss on your own.

2

Clarify

Process each item and AI instantly suggests the right category, project, and context. What used to take judgment calls now takes a single click.

3

Organize

Seven GTD categories, projects, areas, and contexts keep everything where it belongs.

4

Reflect

Guided Weekly Review in six steps. The habit that keeps your system trustworthy.

5

Engage

Work from a clean system. AI considers your context, energy, and deadlines to recommend exactly what to do right now.

Workflows

Built-in GTD rituals.

The workflows that make GTD work aren't afterthoughts here. They're first-class features with dedicated UIs and keyboard shortcuts.

⌘⇧B

Brain Dump

Rapid-fire capture or AI-guided prompts that intelligently probe your projects, areas, and commitments to surface the things you've forgotten. Like having a personal executive coach clear your head.

⌘⇧W

Weekly Review

Six guided steps: process Inbox, review projects, check areas, follow up on delegated items, prune Someday, refresh Next Actions. Jump between steps with Option+1–6.

⌘⇧N

What's Next?

AI analyzes your entire system — deadlines, contexts, energy level, available time — and recommends the single best task to work on right now, with clear reasoning for why. Decision fatigue, eliminated.

⌘⇧P

Pomodoro Focus

Link a task, start the timer, and work. Configurable session lengths, automatic break cycles, and per-task time analytics.

AI-Powered

State-of-the-art AI, woven into every workflow.

TaskBlaster Pro connects to the most capable large language models available — Claude, GPT, and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint — to radically simplify the hardest parts of Getting Things Done. Every AI feature is opt-in, so you're always in control.

Your AI productivity partner

The built-in assistant panel sees your full system — tasks, projects, areas, contexts, deadlines — and uses that context to give advice no generic chatbot ever could. Ask it to break down an overwhelming project into concrete next actions, identify commitments you're neglecting, or just think through a decision with you.

Drop in meeting notes, briefs, or any document and AI instantly extracts a structured project with actionable tasks — work that used to take 30 minutes happens in seconds.

Bring your own API key. Supports Claude, OpenAI-compatible endpoints, and NanoGPT. Keys stored securely in your OS keychain.

  • Brain Dump coach — AI adapts its questions to your specific projects and areas, uncovering open loops and forgotten commitments you'd never surface alone
  • One-click clarification — process your Inbox at lightning speed as AI suggests the right category, project, context, and even subtask breakdowns for every item
  • What's Next? — AI weighs deadlines, context, energy level, and time available to recommend exactly what to work on right now, with clear reasoning
  • Suggest Next Step — when a project stalls, AI analyzes its current state and proposes the concrete next action to get it moving again
  • Document-to-project import — feed it a meeting transcript, project brief, or brainstorm and get a fully structured project with tasks in seconds
  • Always optional — every AI feature has a full manual fallback, and data is only sent to your chosen provider when you explicitly ask

Privacy

Your system. Your Mac. Never the cloud.

TaskBlaster Pro stores everything in a local SQLite database on your Mac. No account, no subscription, no syncing, no telemetry. If you use AI features, data is sent to the provider only when you explicitly ask.

Get Started

Up and running in 60 seconds.

No installer wizard. No account creation. Download, open, and start capturing.

Download the DMG

Grab the latest build above. It's a single 58 MB file.

Drag to Applications

Open the DMG and drag TaskBlaster Pro.app into your Applications folder.

Open with right-click

On first launch, right-click TaskBlaster Pro.app and choose Open. Click "Open" in the dialog. macOS remembers your choice after the first time.

Why the extra step? TaskBlaster Pro is currently in beta and not yet notarized with Apple. Right-click > Open bypasses Gatekeeper for this app only. Alternatively, go to System Settings > Privacy & Security and click "Open Anyway" after the first blocked launch. This will not be needed once the app is signed for release.
System requirements: macOS 13 Ventura or later on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4). Intel Macs are not currently supported.
