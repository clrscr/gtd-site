Installation

Deployed in under 60 seconds.

No installer wizard. No registration server. No license key. Download the binary, mount the disk image, and initialize your command center.

Download the DMG

Acquire the latest binary distribution above. A single 58 MB disk image.

Drag to Applications

Mount the disk image and drag TaskBlaster Pro.app into your Applications directory.

Open with right-click

On first launch, right-click TaskBlaster Pro.app and choose Open. Click "Open" in the dialog. macOS remembers your choice after the first time.

Why the extra step? TaskBlaster Pro is currently in beta and not yet notarized with Apple. Right-click > Open bypasses Gatekeeper for this app only. Alternatively, go to System Settings > Privacy & Security and click "Open Anyway" after the first blocked launch. This will not be needed once the app is signed for release.
System requirements: macOS 13 Ventura or later on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4). Intel Macs are not currently supported.
